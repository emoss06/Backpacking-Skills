Filtering water is critical to any backpackng trip to ensure that you do not contract any waterborne diseases. There are several ways in which to filter water, all of which have some pros and cons.

Pros Cons
Boiling Easy, Reliable, Good for cooking Slow for large quantities, Water will be hot
Hand-Pump Filter Relatively inexpensive Gets tiring, Sometimes the intake gets clogged
Gravity Filter Can do other things while it filters, Can store extra clean water in the filter bladder Can feel slow, hard to fill filter bag in still water
Iodine Tablets Very small and light Leaves a taste in the water
UV Filter Small and fast Requires batteries which gives it a higher risk of failing
LifeStraw Small and easy Takes some practice to use successfully with a water bottle
