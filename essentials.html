These 10 things are essential to have on you at all times.

  1. Navigation - This means a map of the area and a compass.
  2. Lighting - A headlamp is the best, as it doesn't require use of your hands.
  3. Sun Protection - This includes suncreen, a hat, and sunglasses.
  4. First Aid - A first aid kit should include at the very least bandaids, antibiotic oitnment, gauze, burn cream, hydrocortizone for bug bites, antiseptic wipes, moleskin, and scissors
  5. Repair Kit - A simple repair kit includes duct tape and a knife.
  6. Fire - This includes waterproof matches and some form of tinder.
  7. Shelter - The best shelter to carry with you is an emergency blanket. They are super small, but can really save you if you get into trouble.
  8. Extra Food - You should always have at least some snacks on you, and for the trip you should pack one extra day of food per person.
  9. Extra Water - This is pretty self-explanatory, but don't let yourself get too low on water, and make sure you check that the creeks in your area are flowing.
  10. Extra Clothes - Having an extra warm layer and rain gear can save you if the weather changes unexpectedly.
